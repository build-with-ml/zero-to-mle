## How to Read Papers Efficiently

1. **Read the abstract** — understand the contribution in 2 minutes
2. **Look at figures/tables** — often tells the whole story
3. **Read intro + conclusion** — understand the motivation and results
4. **Skip the math** (first pass) — focus on intuition
5. **Deep dive** only if it's directly relevant to your work/interviews
6. **Blog about it** — explaining a paper forces understanding
