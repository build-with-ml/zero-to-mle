# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Sharing your own experience to help others
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Gatekeeping ("you need a PhD to be an MLE")
- Dismissing someone's progress or learning approach
- Spam, self-promotion without value, or off-topic content
- Harassment of any kind

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed appropriate.
