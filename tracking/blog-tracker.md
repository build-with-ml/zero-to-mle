## Content Tips

### What Gets Engagement
1. Visual content (GIFs, diagrams, charts)
2. Contrarian takes with evidence
3. "Here's what I built" with demo links
4. Failure stories with lessons
5. Benchmark comparisons with clear winners

### Writing Checklist
- [ ] Hook in first 2 sentences
- [ ] Clear structure with headers
- [ ] Code snippets (not too long)
- [ ] At least 1 visual/diagram
- [ ] Actionable takeaway at the end
- [ ] Call to action (follow, star repo, etc.)
